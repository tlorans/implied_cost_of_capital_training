## What is Implied Cost of Capital?

The implied cost of capital (ICC) is the discount rate that equates the present value
of expected future cash flows to the current market price of a security.

## Key Concepts

* **Present Value**: The current value of future cash flows
* **Discount Rate**: The rate used to calculate present value
* **Market Price**: The current trading price of the security
